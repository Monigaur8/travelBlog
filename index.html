Hi, I'm Gaurav Kansal!

I am working as a Joint Director (IT) at the National Informatics Centre. During my tenure in NIC, i have worked on multiple technologies and handled important projects including National Knowledge Network (NKN), IPv6 implementation across NICNET, Setting up ANYCAST DNS Services, and wireless services across the NICNET. Currently, I am the technical lead of the Public DNS Project for the Indian IP Space, with an aim of improving the cyber posture of the country.
