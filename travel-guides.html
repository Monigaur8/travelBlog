Welcome to LBSNAA

The Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration (LBSNAA), Mussoorie is a premier training institution for the civil services in India. It is an attached office of the Department of Personnel & Training, Government of India. The Academy is located at Mussoorie, a hill station at a height of little above 6580 ft. It is 35 Kms by road from Dehradun and some 270 kms north of Delhi.
